Our Story

Welcome to ExploreJordan, where we're dedicated to bringing the beauty and wonders of Jordan to the world. Founded with a passion for showcasing the rich cultural heritage, breathtaking landscapes, and unique experiences, our platform is designed to provide a seamless journey for travelers and service providers.

Our Mission

At ExploreJordan, our mission is to revolutionize the tourism industry in Jordan. We strive to create a comprehensive and user-friendly platform that encourages tourism, fosters seamless travel experiences, and supports local service providers.

Why Choose ExploreJordan?

We offer a diverse marketplace for travel agencies, hotels, restaurants, and local vendors to showcase their offerings. Our user-friendly interface allows you to create a profile, track your preferences, participate in a loyalty program, and provide valuable feedback.
